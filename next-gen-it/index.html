What this proves

Audit first. Then tighten the operation.

The goal is not to sell another tool. The goal is to help business leaders understand what they already use, what is redundant, what is risky, and what needs ownership.

  • Document the current technical ecosystem
  • Identify duplicate services and unused subscriptions
  • Clarify system ownership and continuity risk
  • Create a practical remediation runbook
