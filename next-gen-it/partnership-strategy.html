Strategic brief

The
partnership
case

Next-Gen IT sits at the intersection of two growing platforms — Teleo Title and Abode Labs — and a market neither one can reach efficiently on their own: small realty teams in Austin and Central Texas. The domain audit is the entry point. The advisor relationship is the asset.

Neither Teleo nor Abode has a last-mile channel into small realty teams. Next-Gen IT is building exactly that — and the domain audit creates a warm, value-first opening no cold pitch can match.

Ecosystem map

01

Partner structures

Teleo Title

Transaction layer

Teleo needs agent relationships to win closing business. Next-Gen IT already has the warm relationships with small teams that take Teleo months to build cold. The referral fee model is table-stakes — the co-branded audit is the differentiator.

Referral partner — per-transaction fee when your clients choose Teleo for title work
Co-branded audits — Teleo sponsors the domain scan as a value-add for their agent relationships. Teleo funds outreach; Next-Gen IT runs the scans under a joint banner
Warm intro — leverage the Jesica connection before pitching the full program

Abode Labs

Operations layer

Abode is a proptech AI platform that needs small-team adoption in order to grow. They can't cost-effectively reach small realty teams on their own. Next-Gen IT is the boots-on-the-ground partner they need in the Texas market.

Implementation partner — Abode refers new Texas clients to Next-Gen IT for onboarding and support, earning a service fee per engagement
Market development — Abode funds audit outreach in exchange for introductions and market intel; effectively Next-Gen IT as their regional channel
First move — connect with Don directly; Shonna's motivation to grow makes timing favorable

Teleo owns the transaction layer (closings). Abode owns the operations layer (AI workflow, day-to-day ops). They are complementary — not competitive. This is the leverage: Next-Gen IT can present both to the same team as a unified stack. "Teleo for your closings, Abode for your ops, Next-Gen IT keeping your tech infrastructure healthy." No single vendor can offer that. You can.
